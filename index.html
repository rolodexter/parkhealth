Tenets, visions, hypotheses, missions, projects, and values are all related in that they are all part of a larger strategy for achieving a desired outcome. Tenets are the fundamental beliefs or principles that guide a strategy, while #visions are the desired future state that the strategy is aiming for. #Hypotheses are educated guesses based on scientific or logical reasoning that help to guide the strategy, while missions are goals or objectives that the strategy is aiming to achieve. Projects are the tasks or activities that are undertaken to accomplish the mission, while values are beliefs or standards of behavior that guide decision-making. All of these elements are necessary components of any successful strategy.
